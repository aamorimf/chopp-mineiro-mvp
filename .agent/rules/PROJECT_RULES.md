# PROJECT_RULES.md - CRA-Flow Business Rules

## OFFSET RULES

- Offset robot handles only products WITHOUT finishing (initial phase)
- Some orders may not contain finishing block

---

## FINISHINGS TO IGNORE

- DUPLAFACE
- VINCO
- REVISTA
- RECORTE-ELETR_NEW
- VENTAROLA
- FURO
- CORTE
- DOBRA

---

## SIGN PRODUCTS TO IGNORE

- TMFB
- TOXF
- SUBL

---

## PRIORITY RULE

- Priority may appear as ENT-XX or URG
- URG = highest priority (same as ENT)

---

## OPERATIONAL GOALS

- Reduce operator intervention
- Prevent automation errors
- Maintain predictable behavior
